Akron Trade and Transport

Akron Trade and Transport, a full-fledged Tanker Management company and a leading Bunker Fuel Supplier strategically located in the East Coast of UAE at the Arabian Peninsula was established in 1997 and carries out its principal operations from Fujairah, UAE.

Company manages and operates a fleet of oil and chemical tankers of cargo carrying capacity ranging from 1000 MT to Suez max size .

We manage ships efficiently and economically with absolute regard to Health, Safety, Security & the Environment by employing industry Best Management Practices and Risk Analysis Techniques as a tool for self-assessment and continuous improvement ( such as OCIMF TMSA).

Company and fleet Safety Management System are audited, certified and vetted by major IACS classification societies as well as oil majors.
